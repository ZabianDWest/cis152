Activites at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Explore the beautiful coastal trails on your own or join our guided tours. Our knowledgeable guides will lead you through the stunning landscapes, sharing insights about the local flora and fauna along the way. Whether you're a seasoned hiker or a beginner, our trails offer something for everyone.

Kayaking

Experience the coastal waters from a unique perspective as you paddle through crystal-clear waters and explore hidden coves and sea caves. Our guided kayaking tours are suitable for all skill levels, providing a safe and enjoyable way to connect with the natural beauty of the Pacific Coast.

Bird Watching

Observe the diverse array of bird species that call the Pacific Coast home. Our guided bird watching tours will help you identify different types of birds and learn about their behaviors and habitats.
