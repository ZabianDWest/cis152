Enjoy Nature in luxuary

Pacific Trails Resort offers a special lodging experience on the California coast. Our resort is surrounded by natural beauty, with stunning views of the Pacific Ocean and nearby hiking trails. Whether you're looking for a romantic getaway or a family vacation, our resort has something for everyone.

  • Relax in our lodge while enjoying complimentary appetizers and beverages
  • Savour nightly fine dining with an ocean view
  • Unwind in the heated outdoor pool and whirlpool
  • Explore the coast on your own or join our guided tours
